1. INTRODUCTION AND PURPOSE
================================================================================

TechCorp Inc. is committed to providing flexible work arrangements that support
employee wellbeing, productivity, and work-life balance while maintaining
operational excellence and team collaboration.

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work to ensure:
- Consistent standards across the organization
- Clear expectations for employees and managers
- Protection of company assets and data
- Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements

Policy Owner: Human Resources & IT Department
Contact: remotework@techcorp.com | Extension: 5600

2. SCOPE AND ELIGIBILITY
================================================================================

2.1 Who Can Work Remotely?

This policy applies to:
✓ Full-time employees
✓ Part-time employees (20+ hours/week)
✓ Contractors (with manager approval)

Not eligible:
✗ Employees in probationary period (first 90 days)
✗ Roles requiring physical presence (e.g., lab technicians, facilities)
✗ Employees with performance issues

2.2 Role Suitability

Suitable roles for remote work typically:
- Can be performed independently
- Have measurable outputs
- Do not require specialized on-site equipment
- Have minimal in-person collaboration requirements

Managers will assess role suitability on a case-by-case basis.

3. REMOTE WORK ARRANGEMENTS
================================================================================

3.1 Types of Remote Work

TechCorp offers three remote work models:

A) FULLY REMOTE
   - Work from home 100% of the time
   - May be required to attend office for specific events
   - Geographic restrictions apply (must reside in approved states/countries)

B) HYBRID
   - Split time between office and remote location
   - Typical schedules:
     * 3 days office / 2 days remote
     * 2 days office / 3 days remote
   - Core office days may be designated by team

C) AD-HOC REMOTE
   - Occasional remote work for specific needs
   - Does not require formal agreement
   - Subject to manager approval
   - Maximum 2 days per week

3.2 Geographic Restrictions

Remote employees must:
- Reside within the same country as their office location
- Be in a time zone within +/- 3 hours of their team
- Have reliable high-speed internet (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Comply with local employment laws

International remote work requires VP-level approval and legal review.

3.3 Office Presence Requirements

Even fully remote employees may be required to:
- Attend quarterly team meetings
- Participate in annual company events
- Come to office for onboarding, training, or special projects
- Provide 2 weeks notice for required office visits

Travel expenses for required office visits will be reimbursed.

4. EQUIPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY
================================================================================

4.1 Company-Provided Equipment

TechCorp provides the following equipment for remote work:

Standard Package:
- Laptop (Mac or Windows, based on role)
- External monitor (24-inch)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset with microphone
- Laptop stand
- Webcam (if laptop camera insufficient)

Additional equipment (e.g., second monitor, ergonomic chair) may be approved
based on role requirements and manager discretion.

4.2 Employee Responsibilities

Employees must:
- Use company equipment only for work purposes
- Maintain equipment in good condition
- Report any issues immediately via IT helpdesk
- Return all equipment if employment ends or remote arrangement terminates
- Not allow others to use company equipment

4.3 Home Office Setup

Employees should establish a dedicated workspace that:
- Is quiet and free from distractions
- Has adequate lighting
- Is ergonomically sound
- Has reliable internet connectivity
- Is separate from living areas (if possible)

TechCorp provides a one-time $500 home office allowance for furniture and
supplies. Submit receipts to Finance for reimbursement.

4.4 Internet and Connectivity

Minimum requirements:
- Download speed: 50 Mbps
- Upload speed: 10 Mbps
- Latency: < 50ms
- Backup connection available (mobile hotspot)

TechCorp reimburses up to $75/month for home internet costs.
Submit expense claim monthly with internet bill.

4.5 Software and Tools

All remote employees must use:
- Company VPN when accessing corporate systems
- Microsoft Teams for communication
- Email (Outlook)
- Project management tools (Jira, Asana)
- Password manager (LastPass)
- Time tracking software (as required by role)

Personal software installations require IT approval.

5. WORK HOURS AND AVAILABILITY
================================================================================

5.1 Core Hours

All employees must be available during core business hours:
- 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (local time)

During core hours, employees must:
- Be online and responsive on Teams
- Attend scheduled meetings
- Respond to urgent requests within 30 minutes

5.2 Flexible Hours

Outside core hours, employees may work flexible schedules provided:
- Total work hours meet employment agreement (typically 40 hours/week)
- Manager is informed of typical schedule
- Team collaboration is not impacted
- Customer-facing commitments are met

5.3 Time Zone Considerations

Employees working across time zones should:
- Indicate their time zone in email signature and calendar
- Set Teams status to show working hours
- Accommodate critical meetings in team's primary time zone
- Rotate meeting times fairly if significant time zone differences exist

5.4 Overtime and After-Hours Work

Remote employees are not expected to work beyond regular hours unless:
- Explicitly approved by manager
- Emergency situation
- Specific project deadline

Overtime must be pre-approved and will be compensated according to
employment agreement and local laws.

6. COMMUNICATION AND COLLABORATION
================================================================================

6.1 Response Time Expectations

During work hours:
- Urgent messages (Teams): 15 minutes
- Email: 2 hours
- Non-urgent requests: Same business day

Out of office:
- Set Teams status appropriately
- Enable auto-reply on email
- Delegate urgent matters to colleague

6.2 Meeting Best Practices

For virtual meetings:
- Join 2 minutes early
- Use video when possible (exceptions for bandwidth issues)
- Mute when not speaking
- Use chat for questions
- Take notes and share action items
- Record meetings for absent team members (with consent)

6.3 Daily Standup/Check-ins

Teams are encouraged to have brief daily check-ins:
- 15-minute standup or async update
- Share today's priorities
- Identify blockers
- Maintain team connection

Format determined by each team (synchronous or asynchronous).

6.4 Collaboration Tools

Use appropriate tools for different needs:

Quick questions: Teams chat
Discussions: Teams call or video
Brainstorming: Virtual whiteboard (Miro, Mural)
Documentation: Confluence, SharePoint
Project tracking: Jira, Asana
File sharing: OneDrive, SharePoint

7. PERFORMANCE AND PRODUCTIVITY
================================================================================

7.1 Performance Measurement

Remote employees are evaluated based on:
- Output and deliverables (not hours worked)
- Quality of work
- Meeting deadlines
- Team collaboration
- Communication effectiveness
- Adherence to company values

7.2 Productivity Expectations

Managers will:
- Set clear goals and deliverables
- Provide regular feedback
- Conduct bi-weekly 1:1s (minimum)
- Review progress quarterly

Employees will:
- Communicate progress proactively
- Flag issues early
- Ask for help when needed
- Maintain regular communication

7.3 Monitoring and Tracking

TechCorp does NOT use:
- Keystroke monitoring
- Screen recording
- Intrusive surveillance tools

We trust employees and measure results, not activity.

Time tracking is required only for:
- Billable client work
- Project accounting
- Specific compliance requirements

8. DATA SECURITY AND CONFIDENTIALITY
================================================================================

8.1 Security Requirements

All remote employees must:

✓ Use company VPN for all work activities
✓ Enable full-disk encryption on devices
✓ Use strong passwords (12+ characters, password manager)
✓ Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA)
✓ Lock computer when stepping away
✓ Keep software and OS updated
✓ Use encrypted file sharing only (no personal Dropbox, etc.)

8.2 Prohibited Activities

Never:
✗ Access company systems on public Wi-Fi without VPN
✗ Share passwords or credentials
✗ Leave confidential documents visible
✗ Discuss sensitive information in public spaces
✗ Allow family/friends to use company equipment
✗ Store company data on personal devices

8.3 Incident Reporting

Report security incidents immediately to:
- IT Security: security@techcorp.com
- Phone: (555) 123-5700 (24/7 hotline)

Incidents include:
- Lost or stolen equipment
- Suspected data breach
- Phishing attempts
- Unauthorized access
- Malware infection

8.4 Data Classification

Handle data according to classification:

PUBLIC: No restrictions
INTERNAL: Company employees only, VPN required
CONFIDENTIAL: Encrypted storage, need-to-know basis
RESTRICTED: Special handling, approval required

When in doubt, treat data as CONFIDENTIAL.

9. HEALTH AND SAFETY
================================================================================

9.1 Ergonomics

Set up your workspace to prevent strain:
- Monitor at eye level
- Chair supports lower back
- Feet flat on floor
- Keyboard and mouse at elbow height
- Take breaks every hour

TechCorp offers virtual ergonomic assessments. Contact wellness@techcorp.com

9.2 Breaks and Wellness

Employees should:
- Take a 30-minute lunch break (unpaid)
- Take two 15-minute breaks during the day
- Step away from the desk regularly
- Set boundaries between work and personal life

9.3 Mental Health Support

Remote work can be isolating. TechCorp provides:
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 24/7 counseling
- Mental health days (included in sick leave)
- Virtual wellness programs
- Social connection activities

Contact: wellness@techcorp.com or EAP: 1-800-XXX-XXXX

9.4 Workers' Compensation

Injuries sustained during work hours in your home office may be covered by
workers' compensation. Report any work-related injuries immediately to HR.

10. APPLICATION AND APPROVAL PROCESS
================================================================================

10.1 How to Apply

To request a remote work arrangement:

Step 1: Review eligibility criteria
Step 2: Discuss with your manager informally
Step 3: Complete Remote Work Application Form
Step 4: Submit via HR Portal: https://portal.techcorp.com/remote
Step 5: Await approval (typically 2 weeks)

10.2 Approval Criteria

Applications are evaluated based on:
- Role suitability
- Employee performance history
- Team dynamics and coverage
- Business needs
- Technology capabilities

10.3 Trial Period

New remote work arrangements include a 90-day trial period:
- Performance and productivity will be monitored
- Regular check-ins with manager
- Either party can end arrangement with 2 weeks notice
- After trial, arrangement becomes permanent (subject to ongoing review)

10.4 Modification or Termination

Remote work arrangements may be modified or terminated if:
- Performance declines
- Business needs change
- Role changes
- Employee relocates outside approved area
- Security or compliance issues arise

Employees will receive 30 days notice of termination (except for cause).

11. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Can I work from a coffee shop or co-working space?
A: Yes, occasionally, but ensure you use VPN and protect confidential
   information. Do not discuss sensitive matters in public. Regular work
   should be from your designated home office.

Q: What if my internet goes down?
A: Have a backup plan (mobile hotspot, nearby co-working space). Notify your
   manager immediately. If outage exceeds 2 hours, you may need to take PTO
   or work from office.

Q: Can I move to a different state/country?
A: Relocations require HR approval due to tax, legal, and payroll implications.
   Submit request at least 60 days in advance. International moves require
   VP approval.

Q: Who pays for my home office furniture?
A: TechCorp provides a one-time $500 allowance. Purchases above this amount
   require manager approval.

Q: Am I required to have video on during meetings?
A: Generally yes, to maintain team connection. Exceptions for bandwidth issues
   or specific circumstances - discuss with your manager.

Q: Can I care for children while working remotely?
A: Remote work is not a substitute for childcare. You are expected to be fully
   focused during work hours. Make appropriate childcare arrangements.

Q: What happens to my remote work arrangement if I change roles?
A: The new role must be evaluated for remote work suitability. Your arrangement
   may continue, be modified, or end based on the new role's requirements.

Q: Can I expense a co-working space?
A: In some cases, yes. If your home is not suitable for regular work, discuss
   with your manager. Typically covered up to $200/month with approval.
